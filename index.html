About Me

Bayes Business School | Richmond American University | Corvinus University


Welcome! I'm Ben Kiss, a passionate finance professional with a background in investment and financial risk management. With a keen interest in finance and tech, I've honed my skills through a mix of academic studies, internships, and real-world projects.

I hold a BSc in Investment and Financial Risk Management from Bayes Business School, where I developed a strong foundation in financial analysis, valuation, and risk management. In addition, I studied Applied Economics at Corvinus University in Budapest, and Psychology at Richmond, The American University in London. My experiences, ranging from working at the Silicon Cape Initiative to contributing to merger research at the Bayes M&A & PE Society, have equipped me with a unique blend of analytical rigor and entrepreneurial spirit.

As a lifelong learner, I've actively developed my technical skill set, completing coding courses in Python, SQL, and VBA to complement my financial expertise. My aim is to combine these disciplines to craft innovative solutions in the finance world, with a specific interest in building tools that empower decision-making through financial modeling and risk analysis.

I am passionate about building a future that merges finance with technology. I'm currently undertaking a training to become a full-stack developer, with a focus on developing financial models and tools by leveraging data and technology to solve complex challenges in the finance sector.
